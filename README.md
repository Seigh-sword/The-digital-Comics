# 📚 The Digital Comics Library 🦸‍♀️

Welcome to **The Comic Legends GitHub Upload Hub**!  
This is the official space to upload your digital comic creations, panel frames, and creative stories.

> 📝 Before uploading, please read the following rules carefully. Violators will be bonked with a virtual breadstick 🍞🔨

---

## 🚫 Rules 

- ❌ **No Inappropriate Content**
  - No NSFW, violent, hateful, or offensive comics
  - Keep it **kid-friendly and epic**

- ❌ **No Copyrighted Material**
  - Your comic must be **your original work**
  - You can use AI art *with credits*
  - No stolen panels from Marvel, DC, or Grandma’s private sketchbook

- ❌ **No Spam or Junk Files**
  - Don’t upload memes, zip bombs, or files named `lol.js` that break stuff 😑

---

## ✅ Allowed and Encouraged:

- ✅ Original Comics (Hand-drawn, AI-assisted, or digital)
- ✅ Creative storyboards or short strips
- ✅ Collaborations — Team up and create a full arc!
- ✅ Using AI for help is OK **if** you **give credits** (like: "Art assisted by DALL·E" or "AI Prompt: [your prompt]")

---

## 🧠 Comic Naming Tips:

- Use folders like:
  - `my_comic_series/`
  - `chapter1/`, `chapter2/` etc.
- File names: `page1.png`, `cover.jpg`, `credits.txt`

---

## 💌 Submission Format:

- Organize your comic in a folder  
- Include a `README.md` (optional) inside your comic folder
- Add tags like `fantasy`, `action`, `slice-of-life` in a `tags.txt` (optional)
